>The current total number of first round vaccinations is equivalent to 2 people out of every 17 in the state of Ohio having begun their vaccination sequence.

>The current total number of second round vaccinations is equivalent to 2 people out of every 41 in the state of Ohio having completed their vaccination sequence.
